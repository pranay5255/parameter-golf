Study Notes

What Changed

Asymmetric logit rescale

Why It Works

Different scaling for positive and negative logits before softmax, allowing the model to be more confident in predictions while controlling extreme values asymmetrically.

Key Concepts to Understand

  • Asymmetric rescaling
  • Logit manipulation
  • Softcap alternatives
