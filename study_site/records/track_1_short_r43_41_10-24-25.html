What Changed

NorMuon optimizer

Why It Works

NorMuon normalizes Muon updates by their spectral norm, providing more stable training dynamics. Based on the NorMuon paper (arXiv:2510.05491).

Key Concepts to Understand

  • NorMuon
  • Spectral normalization of updates
  • Optimizer stability
