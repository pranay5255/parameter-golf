Study Notes

What Changed

Move bigram hash computation to GPU

Why It Works

Previously computed on CPU. Moving to GPU eliminates the CPU→GPU transfer bottleneck for bigram hash embeddings.

Key Concepts to Understand

  • CPU to GPU migration
  • Host-to-device transfer elimination

▶ PR #216: [New Record] Removing H2D Movement in Bigram Hash Embedding (-0.25s) +31835 / -27 in 9 files — by @dhruvji

This simple optimization moves the bigram hash computation to gpu. It effectively eliminates one H2D transfer per batch. As such, it also simplifies data loader and makes it so there is one fewer tensor to manage in the async pipeline.
